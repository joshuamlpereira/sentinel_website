Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

Sentinel is built on a simple principle: your data stays on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data. This policy explains exactly what Sentinel accesses, how it's used, and where it's stored.

No Data Collection

Sentinel does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data. No information is sent to external servers — ever. The extension does not use analytics, telemetry, or any third-party tracking.

Local-Only Storage

All data — including browsing activity, blocked request logs, AI scan results, user preferences, and your PIN — is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. Nothing leaves your browser.

On-Device AI Analysis

The extension reads website content solely to perform on-device AI safety analysis using Chrome's built-in Prompt API. This processing happens entirely on your device — no content is sent to cloud servers or third-party AI services.

Web History Access

Sentinel accesses web history (URLs) only to display Trust Scores and log blocked requests. This data never leaves your device and is used exclusively for local analysis and display.

Your Data, Your Control

You can clear all stored data at any time through the Admin Dashboard's Export / Data Management section. You are always in full control of what Sentinel stores on your device.
